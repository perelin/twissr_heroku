Is it ... legal?

I have no idea. Twitter says on their developer page :

"Don’t resyndicate data. If your service consumes Twitter data, don’t take that data and expose it via an API, post it to other cloud services, and so on."

Converting your home timeline feed from their API to RSS and providing it back to you might or might not fall under that clause. So, if Twitter is in a bad mood they might disable this app (see '(alpha)'). Sorry.
